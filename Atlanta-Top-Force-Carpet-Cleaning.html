Atlanta Top Force Carpet Cleaning

Atlanta Top Force Services takes pride and shows integrity in all of our Residential and Commercial Carpet Cleaning services. With over 20 years of Carpet Cleaning experience, and highly trained technicians our goal is to provide our customers with the highest level of superior cleaning services. We want the opportunity to educate our customers on how to maintain their homes and businesses on a regular basis when cleaning their Carpets.Atlanta Top Force uses state of the art equipment and technology that will allow your carpets to dry within approximately 2 hours. Our New HydraMaster Titan 575 Truck Mounted system handles all Residential and Commercial cleaning jobs with extreme water recovery for fast drying time. Atlanta Top Force Services only uses Environmentally Safe and Non-Toxic products to ensure a "Cleaner Today for a Healthier Tomorrow." Our non-soap base products will prevent leaving any residues behind that may attract dirt or cause re-soiling while deep cleaning carpets. This helps ensure your carpets to stay cleaner for a longer period of time prolonging the life and look of the carpet.Atlanta Top Force is dedicated to strive for providing Customer Satisfaction. We offer a variety of services such as; Deep Carpet Cleaning, Upholstery Cleaning, Spot and Stain Removal, Pet Odor Removal, Oriental and Area Rugs, Tile & Grout, and Water Damage Restoration. Some of our Premium services include Carpet/Upholstery Protection, Sanitizer, Pet Odor Removal and Deodorizer.Here at Atlanta Top Force Services we want to be the first and last company to clean in your home or business. We offer 24-Hour Emergency Water Damage Restoration service, and 24-Hour Commercial cleaning services to accommodate businesses. Call today and schedule your appointment to get your home or business Carpets Cleaned at 770-864-9983.We are committed to providing excellent results and use a multi-step process to clean your carpets. The technician will pre-inspect your carpets and pre-treat any problematic areas that may be a concern, followed by rinsing and conditioning the carpet to extract dirt and bacteria. Finally, we will manicure your carpet to give it a desired appearance. During this process we will place corner guards in your home to protect your walls when deep cleaning any carpets.Our sophisticated system allows us the capability to use both high and low pressure psi depending on the job to ensure no residues are left behind and to ensure faster drying times. Our premium services will clean and disinfect your carpet, eliminating any bacteria. This process will help remove unwanted odors and germs that may become harmful to your family and pets.Carpets can be a breeding ground for bacteria, therefore we highly recommend your carpets are treated and cleaned professionally at least once a year to prolong the life and look of your carpet. We also recommend you vacuum your carpets frequently to alleviate and reduce soil particles, dust, pet dander and any other dirt build-up that may have been tracked on your carpets. When cleaning spots and spills we recommend you use a non-soap base product to prevent the possibility of re-soiling.We offer premium services including carpet protection, sanitization, and deodorization to keep your carpets looking and smelling great between professional cleanings.
